## Brand guidelines for sponsors

- Logo: minimum 120px width, light and dark variants preferred
- Copy: factual claims only; no superlatives PrimeAxis cannot verify
- Disclosure: all paid placements labeled “Sponsored” or “Paid partnership”
- Prohibited: crypto scams, unverified health claims, malware, adult content
